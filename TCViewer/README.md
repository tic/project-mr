# TCViewer

A lightweight static HTML web app for viewing and managing video footage from your Tesla's dashcam and camera system.

## Features

- **Easy video playback** - View dashcam and camera recordings directly in your browser
- **Simple interface** - Clean, intuitive UI for quick access to your Tesla footage
- **No installation required** - Static HTML app that runs entirely in the browser
- **Fast and responsive** - Optimized for smooth video playback and navigation

## Getting Started

1. Open `index.html` in your web browser
2. Load your Tesla video files
3. Start reviewing your footage

## About

TCViewer makes it easy to review and archive video footage from your Tesla's built-in camera system. Whether you're checking dashcam recordings or monitoring footage, TCViewer provides a straightforward way to access and manage your Tesla videos.
